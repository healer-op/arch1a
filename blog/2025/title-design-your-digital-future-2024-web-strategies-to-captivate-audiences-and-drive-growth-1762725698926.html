1. Prioritize Responsive Design for Seamless User Experiences

With mobile traffic dominating global web use, a mobile-first approach is non-negotiable. Responsive design ensures your site adapts flawlessly to any device, improving user satisfaction and reducing bounce rates. Users expect websites to load in under three seconds. Delays increase bounce rates and harm rankings. Leverage tools like Google PageSpeed Insights to identify bottlenecks. Compress images with WebP format, enable browser caching, and minimize CSS/JS files to accelerate load times. A faster site improves user retention and signals quality to search algorithms.
