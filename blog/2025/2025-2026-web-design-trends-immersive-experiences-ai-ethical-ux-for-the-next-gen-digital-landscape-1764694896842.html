As technology becomes more powerful, ethical considerations in UX design are paramount. Users are increasingly aware of data privacy and manipulative design patterns. Transparency and user control will be key.

  • Dark Pattern Avoidance: Rejecting deceptive design practices that trick users into unwanted actions.
  • Sustainable Web Design: Optimizing websites for energy efficiency and reducing their environmental impact.

Voice User Interface (VUI) Integration

With the continued growth of voice assistants, integrating VUI into web experiences is becoming increasingly important. This includes optimizing content for voice search and designing conversational interfaces.
