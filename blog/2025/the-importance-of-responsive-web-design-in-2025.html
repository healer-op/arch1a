The Importance of Responsive Web Design in 2025

 

Over 70% of internet traffic now comes from mobile devices. If your website isn’t responsive, you’re losing users — and Google rankings.

 

At ArchiaStudio, every website we build is:
✅ Fully responsive across all screen sizes
✅ Lightweight and mobile-optimized
✅ Designed with user intent in mind
✅ Tested for touch-friendly navigation

 

Mobile optimization is now a ranking factor. Investing in responsive design means your business stays ahead of the curve in both performance and visibility.
