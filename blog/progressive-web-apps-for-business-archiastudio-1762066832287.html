What Are Progressive Web Apps?

Progressive Web Apps are web experiences designed to work seamlessly across devices and browsers. They leverage service workers, offline support, and fast loading times to deliver performance and reliability. Unlike traditional websites, PWAs offer app-like features such as push notifications, home screen icons, and instant access, making them ideal for business use cases.

Why PWAs Are Essential for Modern Businesses

Businesses relying on web-based solutions need PWAs to stay competitive. PWAs reduce bounce rates, improve user retention, and ensure consistent performance even in low connectivity scenarios. For example, a retail business using a PWA can offer real-time inventory updates and seamless checkout processes, enhancing customer satisfaction and conversion rates.
