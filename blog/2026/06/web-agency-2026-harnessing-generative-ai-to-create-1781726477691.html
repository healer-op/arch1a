1. AI‑Generated UX/UI in Seconds, Not Days

Generative AI models analyze client briefs, brand guidelines, and user data to produce ready‑to‑implement mockups within minutes. The workflow includes:

  • Natural‑language brief intake (e.g., “Create a bold e‑commerce landing page for Gen Z”).

  • Heat‑map simulations based on target audience behavior.

  • Color palettes that shift according to user mood detection.
  • Micro‑interactions triggered by real‑time user data.

5. Seamless Integration with Existing Toolchains

7. Measurable ROI for Agencies
