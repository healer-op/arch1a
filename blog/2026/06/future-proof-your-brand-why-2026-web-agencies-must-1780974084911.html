2. Sustainable Design Principles That Reduce Carbon Footprint

Eco‑responsible design isn’t a trend—it’s a requirement. Sustainable sites consume less energy, load faster, and rank higher in search engines that prioritize green hosting.

  • Use of vector graphics and SVG icons instead of heavy raster images.
  • Implementation of CSS‑only animations to replace JavaScript‑intensive effects.
  • Adoption of lazy loading and critical‑path CSS for instant page renders.
