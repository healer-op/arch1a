What Are AI-Driven Microinteractions?

These are small, responsive gestures—think button feedback, progress spinners, or contextual tooltips—enhanced by machine learning models that predict user intent and adjust instantly. Benefits include:

  • Increased engagement through instant, relevant feedback
  • Reduced friction for users with diverse needs
  • Data‑backed design decisions that evolve over time
