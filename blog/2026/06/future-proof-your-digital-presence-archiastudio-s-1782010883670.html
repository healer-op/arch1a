1. Embrace Next‑Gen Architecture: Serverless & Edge Computing

  • Deploy code in regions closest to your users for lightning‑fast load times.
  • Eliminate over‑provisioned servers, reducing operating costs by up to 30%.
  • Automated scaling protects against traffic spikes, ensuring uptime.

  • Vite’s instant module hot‑reloading cuts dev time by 40%.

  • Heatmaps and session recordings guide heuristic improvements.
  • On‑page personalization and A/B testing run in parallel with zero friction.
  • Consent‑aware data collection respects privacy while driving insights.

8. Scale with Micro‑Frontends & Container Orchestration

  • Micro‑frontends isolate teams, speeding up deployment cycles.
  • Kubernetes orchestrators manage microservices, guaranteeing high availability.
  • Self‑healing infrastructure reduces downtime and manual intervention.
  • Prometheus & Grafana dashboards keep performance at peak levels.
