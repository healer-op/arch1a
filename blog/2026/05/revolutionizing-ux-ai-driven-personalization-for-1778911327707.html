1. What is the AI Persona Framework?

Think of it as a smart, modular layer that sits on top of your design system. It learns from every scroll, click, and dwell time, then adjusts layout, copy, color palettes, and interactive elements to match the user’s current mood and intent.

3. How It Works

The framework captures behavioral signals, feeds them into a lightweight ML model, and outputs UI tweaks. Engineers embed tiny “persona widgets” in the codebase; designers define style gates and intent rules; and marketers set performance goals.

4. Customizable Persona Layers

  • Emotion detection via micro‑expressions in video feeds.
  • Context awareness (time of day, device type, geolocation).
  • Goal‑oriented paths: lead capture, product discovery, support.
  • Accessibility overrides for inclusive experiences.
