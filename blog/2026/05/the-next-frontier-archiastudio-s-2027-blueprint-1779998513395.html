The Evolution of Web Experiences

Immersive Technologies Defining 2027

Several key technologies will shape the immersive web experiences of tomorrow:

  • Extended Reality (XR) integration for seamless virtual and augmented experiences

  • Spatial computing that adapts to user contexts and environments
  • Multi-sensory interfaces engaging sight, sound, touch, and even smell
