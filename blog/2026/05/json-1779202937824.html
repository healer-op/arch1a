Step‑by‑Step Process: From Concept to Completion

  1. Consultation: Understand your vision, goals, and target audience.
  2. Concept Development: Sketches, mood boards, and style guides.
  3. 3D Modeling: Accurate digital models built from plans or BIM data.
  4. Rendering & Texturing: Apply materials, lighting, and environment settings.
  5. Review & Revisions: Collaborative feedback loop to perfect the visuals.
  6. Delivery: Final assets formatted for print, web, VR, or presentations.

Tips for Getting the Most Out of Your Visuals

  • Provide clear floor plans and elevation drawings.
  • Specify lighting conditions (time of day, weather, interior ambience).
  • Share material samples or mood boards for accurate texture replication.
  • Define the intended audience – investors, clients, or regulatory bodies.
  • Use high‑resolution output for marketing collateral; lower‑res for quick reviews.
