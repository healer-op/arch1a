What is AI-Driven UX?

AI-driven UX refers to the use of artificial intelligence to create personalized, adaptive user experiences. This approach enables websites to learn and respond to user behavior, providing a tailored experience that drives engagement and conversion. With AI-driven UX, small businesses can deliver big-brand experiences without the hefty price tag.
