Web Design Trends to Watch in 2027

This year, we can expect to see a significant shift towards immersive and interactive experiences. Some of the top web design trends include:

  • Augmented reality (AR) and virtual reality (VR) integrations

  • Sustainable and eco-friendly design practices to reduce digital waste
